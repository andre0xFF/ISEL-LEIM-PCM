The Nintendo “Virtual Boy” was released in the United States in 1995 and discontinued in 1996.
The 3D effects are a result of two 1x224 linear arrays, each one directed to an eye that are presented to the player.
Fifteen years later VR is back in a big way with Oculus, Samsung Gear VR, Project Morpheus, Google Cardboard, HTC Vive, OSVR.
